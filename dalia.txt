Ingrediants
1. 500ml milk
2. 30gm sugar
3. 100gm crushed wheat
4. 10 ml ghee

Directions
1. Take the pan. Put the ghee into it and heat it.
2. Now add dalia to the pan, roast it for some time till its
   color becomes brownish.
3. Now take another vessel and pour milk into it.
4. Start heating the milk, after some time add roasted dalia to it.
5. Boil the milk till all the dalia becomes soft.
6. Add sugar to it and server hot.
